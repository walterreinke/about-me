Career Goals:

Politics is one of my passions. Talking about politics is one of the things I enjoy doing most (most of the time). Before coming to college, I was trying to figure out a way to get a political science degree and not have to move back in with my parents after college. I decided that double majoring in journalism and political science would allow me to get paid to talk about politics. I really enjoy making videos, specifically the video editing process, so I would like to go into some form of visual journalism and take part in the video making process, whether that means being the reporter in the video, being a producer, or being an editor. I aspire to work for a company like Vice, that makes videos on national and international politics and also does a lot of great investigative journalism. Andrew Callaghan, who travels around the country interviewing people, is one of my idols and I would love to do something similar to what he does some day. Hasan Piker, a political commentator and streamer, is another one of my idols and has my dream job: getting paid to talk about politics and play video games.
